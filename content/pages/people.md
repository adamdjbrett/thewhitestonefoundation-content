People
======

![profile photo](http://localhost:8080/images/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/VTaylor-1.jpg)

**Victor Taylor** is President of The Whitestone Foundation as well as

Executive Editor for *The Journal for Cultural and Religious Theory*.

He is Director of Graduate and Non-Traditional Programs at York College of Pennsylvania.

![profile photo](http://localhost:8080/images/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/Carl-Raschke.jpg)

**Carl Raschke** is Secretary-Treasurer of The Whitestone Foundation as well as

Senior Editor for *The Journal for Cultural and Religious Theory*.

He is Professor of Philosophy of Religion and Director of the Master of Arts Program in Religious Studies at the University of Denver.

![profile photo](http://localhost:8080/images/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/J-Ramos.png)

**Joshua Ramos** is Special Projects Editor for Whitestone Publications.

![profile photo](http://localhost:8080/images/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/Kev-Grane.jpg)

**Kev Grane** is Assistant Editor for Whitestone Publications. He is also a PhD student at

the University of Denver-Iliff School of Theology Joint Doctoral Program.
